Frequently Asked Questions

What details can I update through Update Aadhaar online Service?
You can update your name, address, date of birth, gender, mobile number, and email address through the Update Aadhaar online Service.
Is there any fee involved for Online updation of Demographics details?
Yes, there is a nominal fee of Rs. 50 for online updation of demographic details (Name, Address, Date of Birth, Gender, Mobile Number, and Email).
How many times Aadhaar data can be updated?
There is no limit on the number of times you can update your Aadhaar data. However, each update request is processed separately and may require appropriate supporting documents.
What changes can I do to my name in Aadhaar?
You can make minor corrections, change the spelling, add or remove parts of your name, or change your name entirely. However, any change in name requires supporting documents as proof.
What documents are required for Online Address Update?
For online address update, you need to provide a scanned copy of a valid proof of address document. Acceptable documents include Passport, Bank Statement, Utility Bill, or any government-issued document with your new address.
